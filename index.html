CV Tips That Actually Work

One page for under 5 years experience. Two pages maximum ever. Name and contact at top. No photo. No "references available on request." Bullet points not paragraphs. Tailor it for every single job — copy words from the job advert into your CV.

⚡ Tailor every application
🎯

Cover Letters — Keep It Short

Three short paragraphs. Why you want this specific job. What you bring. When you're available. That's it. Most cover letters are too long and too generic. Shorter and specific beats longer and vague every time.

✅ 3 paragraphs maximum
🤝

Interview Prep — STAR Method

Situation, Task, Action, Result. Prepare 5 examples from your life that answer "tell me about a time when..." questions. Practice out loud — not in your head. Record yourself on your phone. Painful but it works.

🎤 Practice out loud
💡 The one CV trick that works every time: Copy the exact words from the job description into your CV and cover letter. Applicant Tracking Systems (ATS) scan for keyword matches before a human ever sees your application. If the job says "customer service experience" — use those exact words. Not "worked with customers." Exact words.
